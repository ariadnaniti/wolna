About the founder
Margarita Vasileva
Founder of VOOM
Volunteering has always resonated with me. I was an EVS volunteer at an international project in Poland in 2015 and I not only want to share my experience of participating in the project, but also to give more opportunities to people of different ages and experiences. Around 2014, I began to look for different options for volunteering, both local and international, so I came up with the idea of ​​​​creating this project. I want to give people the opportunity to find their projects, from helping the local community to opening the horizons of international projects.

In 2020, I participated in the Erasmus + for young entrepreneurs. There, for the first time, I was able to start developing the idea and apply for the first grant. In 2022, I won a grant from Atlas Corps to create this site to present the project as a whole.
